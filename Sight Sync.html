Sight Sync

Rules:

  • 1. Each team must consist of exactly two members.
  • 2. The event will consist of a maximum of 3 rounds.
  • 3. There will be no group discussions during the event rounds.
  • 4. The objective of the event is for participants to connect the images logically to arrive at or deduce the correct answer.
  • 5. Each round or the entire event will have a specified time limit for participants to view the images and submit their answers.
  • 6. Participants must submit their answers based on their interpretation of how the images connect.
  • 7. Answers will be judged based on correctness, clarity of reasoning, and the logical connection made between the images.
